## Company Background (from Wikipedia)
Microsoft Corporation is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Redmond, Washington. Founded in 1975 by Bill Gates and Paul Allen, its name is a portmanteau of "microcomputer" and "software". Microsoft rose to prominence in the 1980s and 1990s with MS-DOS and Windows operating systems, and the Microsoft Office suite. Today, it is the world's largest software company by revenue and market capitalization (second-largest overall company). Key products include Windows OS, Office productivity suite, Azure cloud computing platform, Surface hardware, Xbox gaming, LinkedIn, and Bing search. Microsoft has heavily invested in AI through its partnership with OpenAI. Its business model spans software licensing, cloud services (dominant in enterprise), hardware, and subscriptions (Microsoft 365). It competes with Amazon (cloud), Google (search/cloud/office), Apple (OS/hardware), and others in various segments.
