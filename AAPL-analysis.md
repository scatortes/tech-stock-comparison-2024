## Company Background (from Wikipedia)
Apple Inc. is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California. Founded on April 1, 1976, by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne, it designs, manufactures, and markets consumer electronics, computer software, and online services. Apple's hardware products include the iPhone smartphone, iPad tablet computer, Mac personal computer, Apple Watch wearable, and Apple TV digital media player. Its software includes the macOS and iOS operating systems, iCloud cloud service, Apple Music, Apple TV+, and the App Store. Apple is the world's largest technology company by revenue and one of the Big Five American technology companies. As of 2024, it is the fourth-largest company in the world by market capitalization. Apple's business model emphasizes an integrated ecosystem of hardware, software, and services, fostering customer loyalty through seamless integration and premium branding. It competes in smartphones (vs. Samsung, Google), PCs (vs. Dell, HP), and services (vs. Google, Microsoft).
